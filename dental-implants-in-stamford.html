What is the timeline for getting a dental implant at your Stamford office?
The dental implant process at GD Dentistry of Stamford generally takes 3 to 6 months from start to finish. After your initial consultation and 3D imaging, we schedule the implant placement surgery. It usually takes about 3 to 4 months for the implant to fully integrate with your jawbone before the final crown can be attached. We’ll provide you with a clear treatment plan and keep you updated at every stage.
Is GD Dentistry of Stamford licensed and experienced in dental implant procedures?
Absolutely. Our dentists are fully licensed in the state of Connecticut and have advanced training in implant dentistry. What is the process for getting a dental implant with your office?
The process begins with a comprehensive consultation including digital X-rays or a 3D CT scan to assess your bone structure. If you're a good candidate, we’ll schedule the implant placement. After the implant is placed and allowed to heal, we attach an abutment and then a custom-made crown that matches your natural teeth. Throughout the process, all procedures are performed right here in our Stamford office by our experienced team.
