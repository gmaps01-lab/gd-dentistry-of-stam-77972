Frequently Asked Questions About Cosmetic Dentist

How long does it take to get cosmetic dental work done, such as veneers or bonding?
The timeline depends on the procedure. For veneers, the process at GD Dentistry of Stamford typically involves two to three visits over about two weeks: one for consultation and impressions, one for preparation, and a final visit to place the veneers. Cosmetic bonding is often completed in a single visit. After your initial consultation, we’ll outline a clear treatment timeline for your case.
