Frequently Asked Questions About Root Canal Dentist

How much does a root canal typically cost at GD Dentistry of Stamford, and does dental insurance cover it?
The cost of a root canal at GD Dentistry of Stamford can range from $900 to $1,400 per tooth, depending on the complexity and which tooth is affected. Most dental insurance plans cover a portion of the procedure, but coverage varies. Our staff is happy to provide a detailed estimate based on your specific case and will help you understand your insurance benefits before treatment begins. We also offer flexible payment options for patients without insurance.
How long does a root canal procedure take, and how many visits will I need?
Most root canal treatments at our Stamford office are completed in a single 60–90 minute appointment. However, if the infection is severe or the tooth has complex roots, you may need a follow-up visit. Your dentist will review your X-rays and discuss the expected timeline with you during your initial consultation.
Are your dentists licensed and experienced in performing root canals?
Yes, all dentists at GD Dentistry of Stamford are fully licensed to practice in Connecticut and have extensive experience performing root canal therapy. Our team stays current with the latest techniques and technology to ensure safe, effective, and comfortable care.
What is the process for getting a root canal at GD Dentistry of Stamford?
The process begins with a thorough examination and digital X-rays to assess the tooth. After numbing the area, your dentist removes the infected or damaged pulp, cleans and disinfects the root canals, and then seals them. We usually place a temporary filling, and you'll return within a few weeks for a permanent crown to protect the tooth. Our team will explain each step and answer any questions you have before starting treatment.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on root canal treatments?
At GD Dentistry of Stamford, we stand behind our work and offer a one-year warranty on the root canal procedure itself, provided you follow our recommended aftercare and return for your follow-up visits. We use high-quality materials and techniques to maximize the long-term success of your treatment.
