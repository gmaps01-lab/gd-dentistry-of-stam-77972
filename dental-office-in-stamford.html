Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Office

What dental insurance plans do you accept at GD Dentistry of Stamford, and do you offer payment options if I don’t have insurance?

How quickly can I get an appointment for a dental emergency in Stamford?
We prioritize dental emergencies and strive to see patients the same day whenever possible. If you’re experiencing severe pain, a broken tooth, or swelling, call our Stamford office immediately. We reserve several emergency slots each day and do our best to accommodate walk-ins for urgent cases.
Are the dentists at GD Dentistry of Stamford licensed and experienced with cosmetic procedures?
Absolutely. All our dentists are fully licensed in Connecticut and regularly complete continuing education in both general and cosmetic dentistry. Our team has years of experience with services such as teeth whitening, veneers, and Invisalign, and we’re happy to show you before-and-after photos of our work during your consultation.
Can you provide an estimate for the cost and timeline for getting a dental crown?
Most dental crowns at our Stamford office cost between $1,200 and $1,500, depending on the material and insurance coverage. After your initial exam, we’ll provide a detailed estimate. The process typically takes two visits over two to three weeks: the first visit for tooth preparation and impressions, and the second to place the final crown. We use local Connecticut labs for quick turnaround.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on dental work, such as fillings or crowns?
Yes, we stand behind our dental work. Fillings and crowns placed at GD Dentistry of Stamford come with a 1-year warranty covering adjustments or repairs due to normal function. If you have any issues within this period, simply call our office and we’ll schedule a follow-up at no additional charge.
